## SCOPE OF ASME B31.3
Process Piping Code Section B31.3 has been developed considering piping design practices and construction of piping installations found in:
- Petroleum refineries (onshore and offshore)
- Natural gas production facilities
- Food and beverage processing facilities
- Related processing plants and terminals
- Chemical manufacturing
- Pharmaceutical
- Textile and Paper
- Semiconductors
- Cryogenic plants

## OUTSIDE OF THE SCOPE OF B31.3
It is also necessary to note that the code section B31.3 has its limitations. 
- Power Boilers, Pressure vessels, Heat exchangers (except external piping to connect to other equipments): see ASME BPVC Section 1 and ASME B31.1
- Tubes, tube heatres, crossovers and manifolds of fired heaters, internal to the heater enclosure: See ASME B31.1
