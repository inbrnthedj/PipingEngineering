# Cold Spring
- Par. 319.2.4: the intentional deformation of piping during assembly to produce a desired initial displacement and reaction 

In the 2014 edition, when cold spring is used, it is required that the reactions be calculated with:
- **CALCULATION 1**: the assumption that only 2/3 of the design cold spring is present
- **CALCULATION 2**: an additional assessment employing 4/3 of the design cold spring present
