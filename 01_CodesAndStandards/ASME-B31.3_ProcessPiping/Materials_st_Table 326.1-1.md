# ASME B31.3 Nonmetallic Component Standards 
*(Nonmetals and nonmetallic-lined)*
> **Standard Reference:** Table A326.1-1 (Formerly Table A326.1)  
> **Scope:** Chapter VII — Nonmetallic Piping and Piping Lined with Nonmetals

This table lists the officially recognized and approved engineering component standards for plastic, reinforced thermosetting resin (fiberglass), and nonmetallic-lined piping networks. Components built to these standard dimensions and material rules are considered "Listed Components" under ASME B31.3 Chapter VII.

### 💡 Chapter VII Compliance Note
* **Thermoplastic Limitations:** Solvent-cemented joints are explicitly restricted under ASME B31.3 based on toxic/flammable fluid services and must not be used in configurations subject to high external structural moments or vibrations without proper anchoring.
* **Unlisted Nonmetallic Components:** Any nonmetallic component (such as special dual-containment parts or proprietary fiberglass valves) whose dimensional and pressure design standard is **not** inside this table must be fully qualified via **Paragraph A304.7.2** (e.g., destructive burst testing or extensive long-term hydrostatic testing data).
