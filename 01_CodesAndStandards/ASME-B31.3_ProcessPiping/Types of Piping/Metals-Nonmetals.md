# Metallic and Nonmetallic Piping

## Standards
* **Table 326.1.1-1:** METALLIC PIPING COMPONENTS
    - No "A"
* **Table 326.1-1:** NONMETALLIC PIPING COMPONENTS
    - With "A"

## Materials
- Listed and Unlisted pipe and piping components are permitted
- Stress values are provided in Appendix A for LISTED PIPE
- Piping components listed in Table 326.1 may be used in Cat. M fluid service except where restricted in Chapter VIII

## Prohibited products
The following are prohibited:
- single-welded slip-on
- threaded (with some exceptions) joint flanges
- expanded joints
- caulked, soldered, brazed, and bell-type joins

## Examination
* pipe supports are required to be constructed of listed materials
* in the 2016 edition, **100% of circumferential butt welds are to be examined via radiographic or ultrasonic examintations** - a 20% random sample examination is no longer sufficient
