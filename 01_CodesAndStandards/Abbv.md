# Abbreviations

## Scope
This is a collection of different abbreviations I've encountered and looked for. To be reorganized later on.

### English
- ASME: American Society of Mechanical Engineers
    - BPVC: Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code
- ASTM: American Society for Testing and Materials
- API: American Petroleum Institute
- AWS: American Welding Society
- AWWA: American Water Works Association

- HAZOP: Hazard and Operability Study

- IGCC: Integrated Gasification Combined Cycle

- NDT: Non-Destructive Testing
    - UT: Ultrasonic Testing
    - RT: Radiographic Testing
    - MT: Magnetic Particle Testing
    - PT: Liquid Penetrant Testing
    - VT: Visual Testing

- P&ID: Piping and Instrumentation Diagram
- PFD: Process Flow Diagram
- PWHT: Post-Weld Heat Treatment

- PFAS: Per-/PolyFluoroalkyl ("forever chemicals")
- AFFF: Acqueous Film-Forming Foam


### Italian
- CTE: Centrale Termoelettrica (Thermoelectric Power Plant)
- TAF: Trattamento Acque di Falda (Groundwater Treatment Plant/ Groundwater Remediation Facility)
