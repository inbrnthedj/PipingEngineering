## Plants and Processes in the Chemical Industry
The following list contains the main plants and processes in the Chemical Industry. As a Piping Designer, this list will be useful for the interpretation of P&IDs.

* **Processes in the Oil Refinery**
    * **Physical Processes:** Desalting and Dehydration, Crude Distillation, Propane Deasphalting
    * **Thermal Processes:** Visbreaking, Delayed Coking, Flexicoking
    * **Catalytic Processes:** Catalytic Cracking, Catalytic Reforming, Alkylation, Hydroprocessing
* **Production of Light Alkenes**
    * Cracking Reactions (Steam Cracking, Dehydrogenation)
    * Fischer–Tropsch Process
    * Methanol-to-Olefins (MTO) and Methanol-to-Propene (MTP) Processes
* **Production of Synthesis Gas**
    * Steam Reforming Process
    * Autothermal Reforming Process
* **Bulk Chemicals and Synthetic Fuels Derived from Synthesis Gas**
    * **Ammonia:** Ammonia Synthesis Loop, Integrated Ammonia Plant
    * **Methanol:** Methanol Synthesis, Production of Formaldehyde
    * **Urea:** Production of Urea
    * **Synthetic Fuels and Fuel Additives:** * Fischer–Tropsch Process
        * Methanol-to-Gasoline (MTG) Process
        * TIGAS (Topsøe integrated gasoline synthesis) Process
* **Processes for the Conversion of Biomass**
    * Production of Biofuels (Bioethanol, Biobutanol, Biodiesel-Type)
    * Production of Bio-based Chemicals (e.g., Succinic Acid, HMF, FDCA)
    * The Biorefinery (Thermochemical and Biochemical conversions)
* **Inorganic Bulk Chemicals**
    * Sulfuric Acid Production (SO2 Conversion and Absorption)
    * Sulfur Production
    * Nitric Acid Production
    * Chlorine Production (Mercury Cell Process / Electrolysis of NaCl)
* **Homogeneous Transition Metal Catalysis (Bulk Chemicals)**
    * Acetic Acid Production (Methanol Carbonylation / Monsanto and Cativa Processes)
    * Hydroformylation (Oxo reaction of Propene and Higher Alkenes)
    * Ethene Oligomerization
    * Synthesis of Dimethyl Terephthalate and Terephthalic Acid
* **Heterogeneous Catalysis Concepts**
    * Shape Selectivity Processes (Zeolites)
* **Production of Polymers**
    * Processes for the Production of Polyethenes (LDPE, LLDPE, HDPE)
* **Production of Fine Chemicals**
    * **Production Plants:**
        * Multiproduct and Multipurpose Plants (MMPs)
        * Dedicated Continuous Plants (e.g., Acetylsalicylic Acid Production)
* **Biotechnology / Bioprocesses**
    * Fermentation Technology
    * Cell Biomass
    * Enzyme Technology (Biocatalysts, e.g., Production of Mannitol)
* **Process Intensification**
    * Structured Catalytic Reactors (Monoliths)
    * Multifunctional Reactors / Reactive Separation:
        * Reactive Distillation
        * Coupling Reaction and Membrane Separation

*Source: Chemical Process Technology, Second Edition*
